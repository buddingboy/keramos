Paper Presentation

This Technical Session will be devoted to presentation of papers (with oral explanations) and will broadly cover the theme: Traditional Ceramics & Advanced Materials. Those who propose to present papers are requested to send the title and abstract by email to keramos@iitbhu.ac.in before Jan 10, 2016. The subject of the mail should be “Paper Presentation”. Do not forget to mention your full contact details (email ID and phone number) in the email.
The abstracts will be subjected to primary screening by an Expert Committee. Their decision regarding acceptance or otherwise will be communicated to the respective authors in due time.

Rules and Guidelines

1. The abstract has to be not more than 300 words.
2. The abstract should be typed in MS Word and must contain: the title, name of the authors and name of the college/University.
4. The paper must be presented by one of the authors at the time of event.
5. Time limit for each presentation will be 10 + 2 Minutes in which 10 minutes will be given for oral presentation and 2 minutes for question session.
6. Students are requested not to use slides and information from the web. Originality in presentation is desirable.

Technical Quiz

This section tests your knowledge regarding ceramics. Bookish knowledge is something that most of us have. But this quiz asks for more. It asks you to delve deep inside the world of ceramics, and conquer the unknown. So if you think that you have got what it takes, hop on!

Rules and Guidelines

1. Questions will be based on three major categories: Raw Materials (Ceramics), Synthesis & Production (of ceramics) and Application & Usage (of ceramic products)
2. Toppers from individual sections and overall toppers will proceed to the finals
3. Finals will contain question varying from traditional ceramics to advanced materials (covering almost every aspect of ceramics)

Case Study

We will be presenting a case study problem statement from a core industry. The problem statement will be released on the website keramosiitbhu.in and Facebook page of Keramos (facebook.com/keramosiitbhu). The same will be conveyed to you via email or phone.

Rules and Guidelines

1. Teams are to include no fewer than two and no more than five members.
2. Teams must present their case study in the form of a presentation (PPT) or a model or both.
3. Teams have to submit the synopsis of their case study to the judges prior to the start of the event. Entries must contain the following:
a. Cover or title page - Listing Title, College/University and team member’s names.
b. Brief summary - This is a written outline of the case study summarizing all key points. It could include a situation analysis, research, goals and objectives, strategies and tactics, and evaluations. Teams without the synopsis will be disqualified.
4. Only one of the team members can present the case study.
5. Time for the presentation is 10+2 minutes in which 10 minutes will be given for oral presentation and 2 minutes for question session.
